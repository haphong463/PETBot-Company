What is PET plastic bottle?


PET Plastic Bottle (short for Polyethylene Terephthalate) is a type of plastic commonly used to hold liquids and beverages, milk tea, juice, etc. PET plastic is transparent, mechanically durable, and has high heat resistance. .

- Made from 100% virgin pet plastic, not recycled.


- The structure of the plastic circuit remains intact at temperatures of 200°C and – 90°C.

PET is the common name for a type of plastic in the polyester family. Polyester PET is formed from ethylene glycol (EG) and terephthalic acid (TPA) also known as “pure terephthalic acid” or PTA. The full chemical name of PET plastic is polyethylene terephthalate .


- Plastic bottles are sturdy, transparent, the plastic does not look opaque and shiny, those are the bottles without impurities during the bottle blowing process.


- The bottom of the bottle has a clear and transparent PET symbol.

FDA approved safe in food and beverage containers 30 years ago

- Produce according to ISO standards, certificate of food safety and hygiene, food safety and hygiene.
