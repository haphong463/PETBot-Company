Plastic bottles will be very useful if you know how to recycle plastic bottles into useful items

4+ unique plastic bottle recycling ideas and simple ways

1. Ideas to recycle plastic bottles into pen boxes

Parents can use plastic bottles to recycle into pen boxes of different shapes. This both protects the environment and teaches children to be thrifty.

Parents can teach their children how to recycle plastic bottles into pen boxes. This not only makes children excited, but also helps them learn to save and cultivate a sense of environmental protection from a young age. When doing, mothers should choose transparent plastic bottles, cut to the shape that the children like. It can be pictures of funny cartoon characters or animal shapes that your baby wants.

2. The idea or recycling plastic bottles into a multi-compartment, desktop pen box

Recycling plastic bottles into pen boxes is not only simple, but also makes a lot of different designs depending on the purpose you use.

Compared with plastic bottles, the advantages and limitations of glass bottles are as follows:

With too many pens, if you don't like to put them all in the same compartment and want to sort them for easy searching and use, please refer to the following ways to recycle plastic bottles into multi-compartment pen boxes!
First you need to prepare the tools
- Drag.
- Two pieces of cardboard 50x50cm.
- 4 bottles of mineral water 500ml.
- Glue Guns.
- Stickers or paints.
- Drawing pen.
- Compass.
Steps to recycle plastic bottles into pen boxes
- Step 1: You mark the water bottle into many sizes with different lengths according to your wishes and use scissors to cut.
- Step 2: Use a compass to measure into a circle with a diameter for two pieces of cardboard enough to hold 4 water bottles. Once you've cut them, glue them together.
- Step 3: Use colored paper to paste on the round cover, you can choose the color to your liking.
- Step 4: Next, also use stickers, wrap both inside and outside the water bottle or you can use white glue to cover a thin layer and then use paint colors to paint to your liking.
- Step 5: Use a glue gun to glue the bottom 4 of the bottles onto the cardboard and keep them in place when the glue is completely dry. So you have completed the steps to recycle plastic bottles into pen boxes!


3. How to recycle plastic bottles into beautiful containers

Toys on the market are becoming more and more abundant and it is difficult to know what is fake and real. To ensure safety as well as save money, it makes perfect sense to recycle plastic bottles into toys. For example, use colorful bottle caps that stick together to create cute and friendly shapes. Maybe a picture of the sun, a cute puppy image. You can also use plastic bottles to make colorful pig shapes. Cut the bottle into 3 parts, remove the middle, attach the top and bottom of the bottle together. Cork, please punch 2 small rectangular holes parallel to each other to make the pig's nose. Then, use paper to cut 2 ears and attach 2 more eyes. So mom has finished the toys for the baby already! Surely your baby will enjoy this cute pig. Please encourage your child to recycle plastic bottles into their own toys and utensils so that they are creative and unique.

4. Recycle plastic bottles into toys into airplanes

Making airplanes from plastic bottles is also a way to recycle plastic bottles into cool toys for your children. Prepare your baby a plastic bottle of milk that he drinks every day and washes. A ping pong ball, straws, scissors and paper pins. First, please take the scissors to punch a hole in the middle of the bottle cap just enough to be able to plug the straw as the tail of the plane. To make the body, cut off the top of the bottle and cut a short piece to make the tripod. Next, cut the straw into several pieces with a length of 8-10cm. Two parallel segments are used to make propellers, two curved segments are used to make the aircraft's legs, the rest are used to make the tail of the aircraft. The final stage is to use pins to fix the parts of the plane and attach more ping pong balls to make the plane's head. With just a few simple steps, the children already have an airplane in their toy collection.
